Strawberry shortcake

Description

Strawberry shortcake is a classic summertime treat. This delicious strawberry shortcake recipe will be your new go-to for all your strawberry dessert needs.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups fresh strawberries
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 ½ cups whipped heavy cream
  • Salt, baking powder, unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees C. Grease cake pan with butter and dust with flour.
  2. Slice the strawberries and mix with 1/3 cup of white sugar.
  3. Combine flour, 1/4 cup of white sugar, baking powder, and salt, butter with a pastry blender until it resembles coarse crumbs. Add beaten egg, half-and-half, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  4. Stir mixture until just combined and no dry spots remain, being careful not to overmix.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until top is golden brown, about 18 minutes.
  6. Mix whipped cream and cut the cooled cake in half
  7. Arrange ½ of the drained strawberries over bottom layer of cake. Spread ½ of whipped cream evenly over strawberries; place top cake layer over whipped cream.
